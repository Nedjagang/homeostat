# AU-220 — Section 2: Comprehensive (Non-Collision) Coverage

AU-220 covers non-collision physical damage to the insured vehicle, including
theft, vandalism, fire, falling objects, and contact with an animal.

- Deductible: $250 per occurrence.
- Comprehensive coverage excludes damage arising from mechanical breakdown or
  routine wear (see the Wear and Maintenance Exclusion document for AU-220).
