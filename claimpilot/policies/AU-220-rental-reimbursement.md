# AU-220 — Section 6: Rental Car Reimbursement (Premium Tier Only)

For **premium**-tier policyholders, AU-220 reimburses rental car costs up to
$40/day for a maximum of 20 days while the insured vehicle is undergoing
covered repairs following an accident.

Standard-tier policyholders are **not** eligible for rental reimbursement
under AU-220; it would need to be purchased as a separate endorsement.
