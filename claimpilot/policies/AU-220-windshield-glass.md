# AU-220 — Section 3: Glass and Windshield Coverage

AU-220 covers repair or replacement of a cracked or shattered windshield and
other auto glass under the comprehensive portion of the policy.

- Deductible for windshield replacement: **$100**.
- Windshield repair (chip repair, no full replacement) has **no deductible**
  ($0) to encourage early repair over full replacement.
- This clause applies to both standard and premium tiers of AU-220.
