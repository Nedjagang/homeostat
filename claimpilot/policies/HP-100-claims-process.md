# HP-100 — Section 9: Filing a Claim

To file a claim under HP-100, the policyholder must submit a claim through the
ClaimPilot portal or call the claims hotline within 30 days of discovering the
loss. Each claim is evaluated independently against the specific coverage
section it falls under; this document describes the filing process only and
does not itself determine coverage, limits, or payout amounts for any claim.
