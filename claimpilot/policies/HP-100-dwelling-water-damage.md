# HP-100 — Section 2: Water Damage (Dwelling)

Policy HP-100 covers sudden and accidental water damage to the dwelling caused by
the abrupt failure or burst of a plumbing, heating, or air-conditioning system
located inside the insured premises.

- Deductible: $500 per occurrence.
- This clause applies to the dwelling and permanently installed fixtures only.
- Gradual leaks, seepage, or damage occurring over a period of more than 14 days
  are excluded (see the Wear and Maintenance Exclusion document).
