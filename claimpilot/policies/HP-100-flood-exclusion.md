# HP-100 — Section 5: Flood Exclusion

Damage caused directly or indirectly by flood — including surface water,
rising water, storm surge, overflow of a body of water, and water that backs
up through sewers or drains from an external flood event — is **excluded**
from all tiers of HP-100.

Flood coverage is not available as an endorsement under HP-100. A separate,
dedicated flood policy is required for this peril.
