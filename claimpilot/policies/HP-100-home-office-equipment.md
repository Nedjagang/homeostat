# HP-100 — Section 4: Home Business Equipment (Premium Tier Only)

For **premium**-tier policyholders, accidental physical damage to home office
equipment (desks, monitors, computers, and dedicated business furniture used
for remote work) is covered up to $5,000 per occurrence, with a $250
deductible.

This coverage is **not included** in the standard tier of HP-100. Standard-tier
policyholders would need the Home Business Endorsement (sold separately) to
obtain this coverage.
