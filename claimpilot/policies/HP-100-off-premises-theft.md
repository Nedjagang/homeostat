# HP-100 — Section 3: Off-Premises Personal Property Theft

Personal property owned by the insured is covered against theft even when the
property is away from the insured premises (off-premises), including bicycles,
sporting equipment, and personal electronics.

- Off-premises theft coverage is capped at 10% of the total personal property
  limit stated on the declarations page.
- A police report is required within 5 days of discovery for any off-premises
  theft claim.
- This clause applies to standard and premium tiers alike.
